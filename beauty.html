Makeup Trends to Freshen Up Your Look

Monochromatic Makeup

When creating a monochromatic makeup look, follow the rule "same, but different".Monochromatic is one of the easiest make-up looks to create that in its simplicity, has the power to reduce your age by years. We get that the word itself is kind of daunting and long, leaving the impression that it would take expert-level artistry and years of experience to master. But fear not! Because this shade matching trend is taking the world by storm and all you need is a few signature products!

Metallic Eyes

Eyes look mesmerizing thanks to a iridescent shadow all around her upper and lower lids. It's made even more dramatic by lush lashes and black eyeliner.At the end of the day, achieving the radiant glow of your dreams is all about having a skin-first, makeup-second mindset.Put down that tired neutral palette, and pick up a pop of color. Start with your eyes. "Neon eyes are a good way to embrace color with open arms," Sir John says. "Test the waters with a bold liner or a colorful mascara."

Subtle Smokey Eyes

Soften dramatic smokey eyes for daytime by adding a touch of shimmer to inner corners with a gorgeous gold shadow or silver shadow. Keep the rest of your makeup minimal.Breuchaud believes a strong brow is always in style, but we'll be seeing them a lot more in 2019.
